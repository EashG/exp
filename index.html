About The MadOven

Founded by Sarah Johnson in 2018

The MadOven began as a dream to bring artisanal baking to our community. What started in a small kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery where traditional recipes meet creative innovation.

We believe that great baking starts with great ingredients. That's why we source locally whenever possible and never compromise on quality. Every loaf, pastry, and cake is made with the same care and attention we'd give to treats for our own family.

Come visit us and taste the difference that passion makes!
